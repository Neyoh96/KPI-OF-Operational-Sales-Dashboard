Sales Dashboard Power BI Project Report.
 Project Overview

The project aims at creating an Operational Sales Dashboard with Microsoft Power BI to examine the sales performance, customer behavior, operational measurements in many dimensions including time, store, product, gender, and occupation.
It allows stakeholders to make informed decisions based on data through the dashboard that gives a clear and interactive display of business performance.

 Objectives

Track performance of sales in general and the main KPIs.

Understand customer buying patterns.

Determine the best performing stores, products and customers.

Offer handover insights in interactive visualizations.

 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs).

The KPIs that are identified in the dashboard include:

Total Sales: 14M

Total Quantity Sold: 8K

Total Transactions: 2K

Unique Customers: 36

Average Spend per Purchase: 6.32K

These KPIs provide a fast-track picture of business wellness and customer worth.

 Dashboard Insights
Sales Trend Analysis

Total Sales by Calendar year indicates that there is a decline between 2022 and 2023 which reflects potential changes in the market or decreased demand.

The monthly customer trend indicates a slow rise in unique customers with time and this indicates better interaction with customers.

Product Performance

All sales are dominated by electronics as opposed to other products such as Clothing.

MacBook, iPhone, and Samsung are high-performing products that have a significant contribution to revenue.

Store Performance

The highest performing store is Costco then Best Buy and Target.

Store analysis aids in finding out areas where sales strategies are not working.

Customer Demographics

The female customers make a little more contribution to the total sales than the male customers.

Profession-based analysis indicates exhibits good sales:

Engineers

Sales Representatives

Lawyers

Managers

Software Developers

This implies increased purchasing power on the professional segments.
